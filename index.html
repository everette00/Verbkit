Welcome to Verbkit - 2D Game Development Simplified

Verbkit is a tool for simplifying the development of 2D games. Verbkit leverages Typescript and pixijs to remove much of the work needed to build a 2D game, and is comprised of several components to give you building-blocks for building games.

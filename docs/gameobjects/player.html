inputMovment(x:number, y:number, withKey:number, timeSpan:number = 0):Player

Sets movement to a given key.

x: the amount of x-offset per update that this movement applies to the Player

y: the amount of y-offset per update that this movmenet applies to the Player

withKey: the key that applies this movmenet.

timeSpan: the time it takes the Player to move the x-, y-offset in milliseconds.

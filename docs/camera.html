Camera

Overview

A camera is for following an object. There are a few options for how the camera should follow an object.

Functions

focusOn(gameObject:GameObject):Camera

Tells the camera what it should follow

zoomIn(zoom:number):Camera

Has the camera zoom in by a specified value (in percentage, i.e, .5 is 50% more zoomed)

zoomOut(zoom:number):Camera

Has the camera zoom out by a specified value (in percentage, i.e, .5 is 50% more zoomed out)

centerCamera():Camera

Puts the center of the camera on the center of the focus.

leashCamera(width:number, height:number):Camera (not working)

The camera will stay where it is until the focus moves outside of the freedom of the camera. For example, leashCamera(5, 0) will give the focus five pixels of horizontal freedom before the camera begins to move with it.

verticallyLocked():Camera

The camera will remain locked on a horizontal "rail", preventing it from moving up and down.

horizontallyLocked():Camera

The camera will remain locked on a vertical "rail", preventing it from moving left and right.
